Powerful Features for Modern Restaurants

Everything you need to manage your restaurant efficiently, all in one platform.

Core Features

Complete Restaurant Management

From orders to analytics, we have got you covered.

Order Management

Handle dine-in, takeaway, and delivery orders from a single dashboard. Real-time order tracking, status updates, and kitchen display integration.

POS & Billing

Fast, intuitive point of sale system. Generate bills instantly, split payments, apply discounts, and manage cash registers with ease.

Menu Management

Create and organize your menu with categories, variants, and add-ons. Update prices, availability, and descriptions in real-time.

Table Management

Visual table layout, real-time availability tracking, and seamless table assignments. Perfect for dine-in operations.

Reservations

Accept online table bookings, manage time slots, send automatic confirmations, and reduce no-shows with reminders.

Kitchen Display

Digital kitchen display system for efficient order preparation. Prioritize orders, track cooking time, and reduce errors.

Team Management

Staff & HR Features

Complete workforce management for your restaurant team.

Attendance Tracking

Daily check-in and check-out tracking. Monitor working hours, late arrivals, and overtime automatically.

Leave Management

Staff can request leaves online. Managers can approve or reject with one click. Track leave balances easily.

Salary Management

Calculate salaries based on attendance, deductions, and bonuses. Generate payslips and track payment history.

Role-Based Access

Define roles like Manager, Kitchen, Cashier, Waiter, and Delivery. Control what each role can see and do.

Task Assignment

Assign tasks to staff members, set deadlines, and track completion. Keep your team organized and productive.

Performance Reports

Track individual and team performance. Identify top performers and areas for improvement.

Inventory & Finance

Stock & Financial Control

Keep track of your inventory and finances with precision.

Inventory Tracking

Real-time stock levels, automatic updates when orders are placed, and low stock alerts to prevent stockouts.

Supplier Management

Manage vendor information, contact details, and purchase history. Build better supplier relationships.

Purchase Orders

Create and track purchase orders. Receive stock, verify quantities, and maintain accurate records.

Expense Tracking

Record all business expenses by category. Upload receipts, approve expenses, and track spending patterns.

Profit & Loss

Automatic P&L statements. See revenue, costs, and profits at a glance. Make informed business decisions.

Financial Reports

Detailed daily, weekly, and monthly reports. Export to PDF or Excel for accounting and tax purposes.

Online Presence

Customer-Facing Features

Tools to attract and retain customers.

Restaurant Website

Each restaurant gets their own beautiful, responsive website. Showcase your menu, accept orders, and take reservations.

Online Ordering

Customers can browse your menu and place orders online. Support for dine-in, takeaway, and delivery options.

Payment Gateway

Accept payments via bKash, Nagad, and SSLCommerz. Configure your own payment credentials.

Promo Codes

Create discount codes for marketing campaigns. Set percentage or fixed discounts, usage limits, and validity periods.

Customer Reviews

Collect and manage customer feedback. Respond to reviews and showcase your ratings to build trust.

Notifications

Email and push notifications for orders, reservations, and updates. Keep customers informed at every step.

Analytics & AI

Insights & Intelligence

Data-driven tools to grow your business.

Dashboard Analytics

Real-time overview of sales, orders, and key metrics. Visual charts and graphs for quick insights.

Sales Reports

Detailed sales analysis by product, category, time period, and order type. Identify trends and opportunities.

Customer Insights

Understand your customers better. Track order history, preferences, and lifetime value.

AI Assistant

Powered by Gemini AI. Get instant answers to questions, automate customer support, and improve efficiency.

Demand Forecasting

AI-powered predictions for busy periods. Optimize staffing and inventory based on expected demand.

Smart Recommendations

AI suggests menu optimizations, pricing strategies, and operational improvements based on your data.
