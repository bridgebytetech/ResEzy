How Resezy Works

Get your restaurant online in just a few simple steps. No technical knowledge required.

Step 1

Create Your Account

Sign up in under 2 minutes. Just enter your basic information and restaurant details to get started with your free trial.

Quick registration - Only email and password needed
14-day free trial - No credit card required
Instant access - Start using immediately after signup

Quick Setup

Under 2 minutes

Step 2

Set Up Your Restaurant

Configure your restaurant profile with your branding, location, business hours, and contact information. Make it uniquely yours.

Custom branding - Upload logo, set colors and theme
Business hours - Set opening times for each day
Location setup - Add address and delivery zones

Your Brand

Custom styling

Step 3

Add Your Menu

Create categories and add your menu items with photos, descriptions, prices, and variants. Our intuitive interface makes it easy.

Bulk upload - Import menu from spreadsheet
Product variants - Sizes, add-ons, and modifiers
Photo gallery - Multiple images per item

Menu Items

Easy to manage

Step 4

Configure Payments

Connect your payment accounts to receive payments directly. We support bKash, Nagad, SSLCommerz, and cash payments.

Your own accounts - Payments go directly to you
Multiple options - bKash, Nagad, Cards, Cash
No commission - We don't take any cut from sales

Secure

Direct payments

Step 5

Go Live & Start Selling

Your restaurant website is ready. Share your link, start taking orders, and manage everything from your dashboard.

Instant website - Your own restaurant page
Real-time orders - Notifications for new orders
Full dashboard - Manage everything in one place

You're Live!
