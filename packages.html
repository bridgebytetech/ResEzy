FAQ

Pricing Questions

Common questions about our pricing and plans.

Can I switch plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the new rate applies from the next billing cycle.
Is there a setup fee?
No, there are no setup fees for any plan. You only pay the monthly or yearly subscription fee. For Enterprise customers, we offer free onboarding and training.
Do you take commission on sales?
No, we don't take any commission on your sales. Payments from customers go directly to your account. You only pay the fixed monthly subscription fee.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bKash, Nagad, and bank transfers for subscription payments. For Enterprise plans, we also offer invoice-based billing with flexible payment terms.
What happens when my trial ends?
After your 14-day trial, you can choose to subscribe to a paid plan or continue with our Free plan. Your data will be preserved, and you can upgrade anytime.
