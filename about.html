Our Story

From Idea to Innovation

Resezy was born out of a simple observation: restaurant owners spend too much time on administrative tasks and not enough time creating great dining experiences.

We saw restaurant owners struggling with paper-based systems, juggling multiple apps for different tasks, and losing money due to inefficiencies. We knew there had to be a better way.

In 2024, our team at Bridge Byte Tech set out to build a comprehensive solution that would address all the challenges restaurants face - from order management and POS to staff scheduling and inventory control.

Today, Resezy serves over 500 restaurants across Bangladesh, processing thousands of orders every day and helping restaurant owners save time and grow their businesses.
