How quickly can I get started?
You can set up your restaurant on Resezy in under 30 minutes. Our intuitive interface guides you through the process step by step.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.
What payment methods do you support?
Resezy supports bKash, Nagad, SSLCommerz for card payments, and cash. Each restaurant configures their own payment accounts.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade security with encrypted data storage and secure connections. Your data is backed up regularly.
